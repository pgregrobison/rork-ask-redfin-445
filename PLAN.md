# Add splash screen with Redfin logo on app launch

**Features**
- A splash screen appears the moment the app opens, then smoothly fades into the main app after a brief moment.
- The splash uses the same red as the app icon background, so the launch feels like a continuation of tapping the icon.
- A centered white "Redfin" wordmark logo sits in the middle of the screen with a subtle scale-in animation for a polished feel.

**Design**
- Full-bleed Redfin red background (matching the app icon exactly).
- Centered white "Redfin" wordmark, sized for clear legibility on all devices.
- Logo gently scales up and fades in on launch, then the whole splash fades out into the main app after roughly one second.
- Status bar content uses light style while the splash is visible.

**Where it appears**
- Shown once at app launch, before the main tab interface is revealed.
